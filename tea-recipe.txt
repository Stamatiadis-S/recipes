Ingredients:
 - 4 cups water
 - 3/4 cup brown sugar
 - 1/4 cup grated ginger root
 - 3 tea bags
 - 2 lemons, juiced
 - 3 tablespoons honey

 Directions:
  1. Stir water, brown sugar, and grated ginger root together in a saucepan;
     bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook at a simmer for
     20 minutes.

  2. Remove saucepan from heat and add tea bags; steep tea to desired strength,
     3 to 5 minutes. Remove and discard tea bags.

  3. Stir lemon juice and honey into the tea; strain into a pitcher.
